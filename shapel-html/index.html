About Us

Mahmoud Al-Masri the managing director of Spring Design have many years of experience and is one of the top interior designers in the gulf region. He established one of UAE's leading interior design companies. Catering not only to Abu Dhabi but also all of the UAE, offering a complete interior design and interior decorating service. The service Spring Design provides is very much like one stop shopping without the hassles and stress. We can help you as much or as little as you would like us to. We will give you our expert opinion on important building decisions such as paint colors, cabinetry finishes and tile selections, through to the supply of furniture, soft furnishings, home automation, interior and exterior lighting, water proofing and insulation, glass bullet proofing and window treatments and accessories.
